# Code Of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want the `ivn` community to be welcoming, respectful, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in discussions, reviews, and issue threads
- Assume good intent and give actionable feedback
- Focus on ideas, code, and behavior rather than personal attacks
- Help keep the project useful for contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal insults or bad-faith attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive or abusive conduct in project spaces

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, remove content, or restrict participation when behavior harms the community or the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately through the repository owners on GitHub instead of opening a public issue.

When possible, include:

- Links to the relevant discussion, issue, pull request, or commit
- A short description of what happened
- Any context that would help maintainers investigate quickly

Maintainers will review reports promptly, keep reporter details as private as practical, and decide on an appropriate response based on the available evidence.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Depending on severity, maintainers may:

- Ask for a change in behavior
- Remove content or lock threads
- Temporarily restrict participation in project spaces
- Permanently ban repeat or severe offenders

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces such as issues, pull requests, discussions, and other official community channels for `ivn`.
